# 5. 🧟 ENEMY SYSTEM

## 5.1 Enemy Types

* Walk normally
* Flip when hit
* Recover after delay

### Sidesteppers (Crabs)

* Require multiple hits
* Speed increases after first hit

---

### Fighter Flies

* Jump in arcs
* Harder to predict

---

### Slipice (Later Levels)

* Freezes platforms
