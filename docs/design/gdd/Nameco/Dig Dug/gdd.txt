## 1.2 Player Fantasy

You are a digger eliminating underground enemies by inflating them or crushing them with falling rocks while carving your own paths.

---

# 2. 🎮 CORE GAMEPLAY LOOP

1. Spawn underground
2. Dig tunnels
3. Locate enemies
4. Inflate enemies or drop rocks
5. Avoid enemy contact/fire
6. Clear all enemies → next round
7. Difficulty increases
