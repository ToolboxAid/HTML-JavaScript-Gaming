Play as the Simpson family (Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa) fighting through Springfield to rescue Maggie from Mr. Burns.

1. Enter stage
2. Move right through environment
3. Enemy wave spawns
4. Defeat all enemies
5. Advance to next segment
6. Interact with environment
7. Fight boss
8. Progress to next level
