## Base penguin style

Use this as the shared foundation for all heroes:

**Species/style**

* Stylized arcade-cartoon penguin
* Round but athletic body
* Short legs, wide belly, strong readable silhouette
* Cute at first glance, but competitive and slightly intense
* Designed for fast gameplay readability, not realism

**Body proportions**

* Large head and upper torso
* Small flippers, but expressive
* Short stout legs with webbed feet
* Belly-forward stance for comedic momentum
* Slightly oversized beak for expression clarity

**Face**

* Bold eyes, expressive brows
* Determined, mischievous, or cocky expression
* Small blush/cheek color optional for charm
* Beak should support “angry”, “smug”, and “shouting” poses well

**Texture/material**

* Smooth, clean feather shapes
* Not fluffy-realistic
* Bright game-ready shading
* Slight icy rim light
* Feet and beak should be saturated enough to pop against snow/ice

**Movement personality**

* Built for slipping, sliding, bumping, bouncing
* Slight forward lean like a character always about to lose traction
* Strong readability in dash, belly-slide, spin, jump, and knockback poses

**World fit**

* Lives on a floating unstable iceberg
* Arena-combat / king-of-the-hill vibe
* Silhouette must read well over blue-white ice backgrounds

## Overall visual direction

A good top-level art prompt would be:

> A stylized multiplayer arcade penguin character for a chaotic iceberg arena game, cartoon proportions, bold silhouette, expressive face, clean game-ready shapes, slippery physics energy, colorful hero design, readable from a distance, dynamic pose, icy action-game aesthetic.

## Shared hero rules

Because your GDD says each hero is unique and locked once selected, each penguin should have:

* a unique color accent
* a unique head/gear shape
* a unique ability silhouette
* a unique stance/personality read at a glance 

Good design rule:

* same core penguin body
* different accessories, markings, posture, and VFX identity

## Hero-by-hero image briefs

### Splash — Pucker Blaster

Ability is a continuous water push stream.  

**Look**

* Aqua-blue accent colors
* Goggled or playful water-blaster look
* Beak shaped or posed like blowing/puckering
* Small tank, nozzle, or pressure-pack aesthetic
* Fun, cheeky, agile

**Pose**

* Leaning forward blasting a stream of water
* Webbed feet sliding backward from recoil

**Personality**

* Playful troublemaker
* Likes disrupting everyone else

---

### Frostbite — Freeze Blast

Ability creates a temporary freeze zone.  

**Look**

* Cyan / icy white / pale blue palette
* Sharp angular ice motif
* Frost crystals on shoulders, headpiece, or flippers
* Colder, more focused face
* Slight mage / ice-controller vibe

**Pose**

* One flipper extended creating an icy blast or freezing ring
* Frost mist curling around feet

**Personality**

* Calm, precise, dangerous
* Tactical controller

---

### Glacier — Mega Weight

Ability increases tilt influence through heavy mass.  

**Look**

* Largest-looking penguin
* Dark navy, glacier-blue, stone-gray palette
* Thick chest and belly
* Heavy armored ice plates or rugged frozen-rock styling
* Dense and imposing

**Pose**

* Stomping down hard
* Ice cracking under feet
* Strong planted stance

**Personality**

* Slow powerhouse
* Immovable tank

---

### Dash — Slipstream Dash

Ability is burst movement plus knockback.  

**Look**

* Sleek aerodynamic shape
* Bright yellow, electric blue, or neon accent colors
* Speed lines, scarf, visor, or racer markings
* Narrower body language than other penguins

**Pose**

* Mid-dash with trailing ice streaks
* Leaned hard forward, one foot skidding

**Personality**

* Cocky speedster
* Hard to pin down

---

**Pose**

## Strong shared pose ideas for the image engine

## Art-direction constraints to include

Add these to any prompt:

* no realistic feathers
* no photorealism
* no soft plush toy look
* prioritize gameplay readability
* strong silhouette separation
* clean character-select style
* bright but icy palette
* suitable for 2D or 2.5D arcade game art
