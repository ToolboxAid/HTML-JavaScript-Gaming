No-code engine direction

Zero user code, all config and manifest-driven behavior.

The long-term mission is to enable anyone to create games for the open web without writing code.

Build

Create games through configuration, content and composition.

Play

Test, preview, run, discover and enjoy games and examples.

Share

Publish games, assets, templates, tutorials, ratings and community knowledge.

Platform Progression

From early foundations to launch readiness.

Each stage represents a communication milestone for the whole platform experience.

  1. Stage 1 Alpha

    Core concept validation and first connected surfaces.

  2. Stage 2 Beta

    Broader tool hardening and content structure.

  3. Current Stage Developer Preview

    Workspace integration, creator flow polish and platform IA.

  4. Next Stage Public Preview

    Public-facing onboarding, community entry points and release notes.

  5. Stage 5 v1 Launch

    Launch-ready creator destination with mature loops.
