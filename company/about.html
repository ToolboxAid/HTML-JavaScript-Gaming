About Game Foundry Studio

Build, play and share open-web games with no coding required.

Game Foundry Studio is a configuration-driven creator destination where game ideas move from a Game Design Document (GDD) to structured game data and ultimately to playable experiences. Creators build content, rules, worlds, characters, assets, audio, and publishing workflows through visual tools and configuration, with no coding required. Advanced creators can extend projects with custom enhancements and scripting when additional flexibility or control is needed.

Build

Create games through configuration, content and composition: scenes, sprites, rules, inputs, objectives and templates that the engine can understand.

Play

Test, preview, run, discover and enjoy games and examples in the browser while the same configuration-driven foundation powers each experience.

Share

Publish games, assets, templates, tutorials, ratings and community knowledge so every useful pattern can help the next creator move faster.
