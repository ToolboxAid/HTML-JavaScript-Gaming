Pong

Standalone paddle-and-ball arcade set with four rule variants: Tennis, Hockey, Handball, and Jai-Alai. Keyboard and gamepad both drive the same player controls, and paddle motion adds english to every return.

Keys

Arrow Keys or WASD move the paddle. Enter or Space starts, serves, and restarts. Esc returns to the mode menu. A standard gamepad uses the left stick or D-pad plus A to confirm and B to go back.

Gameplay Loop

  • Tennis: classic player-vs-opponent Pong race to seven.
  • Hockey: free-move paddles, open goal mouths, and puck-style rebounds.
  • Handball: attack the front wall, score on clean strikes, survive three misses.
  • Jai-Alai: faster handball with stronger english and tighter control windows.
