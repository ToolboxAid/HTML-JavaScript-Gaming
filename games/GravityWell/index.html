Gravity Well

Vector-space flight game built around orbital pull. Rotate, thrust, and slingshot through gravity wells to collect all beacons without crashing into a planet or drifting out of bounds.

Enter starts or restarts. Left/Right rotate, Up thrusts, and Space applies a braking burn.

Gameplay Loop

  • Read the gravity field and build orbital speed with thrust.
  • Collect every beacon in one run.
  • Avoid planetary collision and hard drift beyond the play area.
