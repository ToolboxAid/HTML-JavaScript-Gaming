AI Target Dummy

AI behavior lab focused on deterministic state transitions: idle, chase, attack, cooldown, and recover.

Controls

WASD/Arrows move target. Enter/Space start. P pause. R reset.

Focus

This build validates AI sensing radius, pursuit behavior, attack timing, and disengage logic with readable debug output.
