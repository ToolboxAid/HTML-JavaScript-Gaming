Thruster

A compact inertial ship-control game: rotate, burn thrust, drift through a bounded chamber, and manage momentum without shooting or gravity fields.

Controls

Left/Right or A/D rotate. Up or W applies thrust. Space or Enter starts. P pauses. R resets. Gamepads use the left stick or D-pad to rotate and the south button to thrust/start.

Gameplay Loop

Start a run, build velocity with short burns, and keep the ship under control inside the arena. This build is intentionally small and does not include shooting, orbit mechanics, or gravity wells.
