Space Invaders

Classic-style alien formation shooter with disciplined single-shot play, descending ranks, lives, score, and wave progression.

Keys

Left/Right or A/D move, Space fires, Enter starts, P pauses/resumes, and X returns to menu while paused.

Gameplay Loop

  • Clear the marching formation before it reaches the player line.
  • Manage one player shot at a time while dodging alien fire from the lowest invaders.
  • Survive across waves as the march cadence tightens and the formation becomes more dangerous.

Audio

This build uses the existing classic-style sound effects already stored in /games/SpaceInvaders/assets/effects.
