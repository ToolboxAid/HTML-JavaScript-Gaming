Paddle Intercept

A bounded one-ball interception game where the paddle predicts the future arrival lane using time-to-intercept math and wall-aware Y reflection.

Controls

Space or Enter starts. P pauses. R resets. Gamepads use the south button to start, Start to pause, and B/East to reset.

Gameplay Loop

Watch the ball bounce through the chamber while the paddle solves a future intercept point on the right side. The green marker shows the current predicted impact height.
