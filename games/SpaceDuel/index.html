Space Duel

Arcade-style vector combat with inertial thrust movement, rotating ships, enemy splits, and same-screen one or two player play.

Keys

1 starts one-player, 2 starts two-player, P pauses, and X returns to menu while paused.

Player 1: Left/Right rotate, Up thrust, Space fire.

Player 2: A/D rotate, W thrust, Left Shift fire.

Gameplay Loop

  • Clear geometric enemy waves while managing inertia and wrap-around movement.
  • Large enemies split into smaller, faster threats when destroyed.
  • Survive hazards, build score, and push into harder waves.

Audio

This build uses generated placeholder WAV files under /games/SpaceDuel/assets/effects for easy asset replacement later.
