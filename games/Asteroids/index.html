Asteroids

Playable Asteroids-style arcade game with player-select flow, high score, player scores, and ship-icon lives.

`1` or `2` starts one-player or two-player. `Left/Right` rotate, `Up` thrust, `Space` fire, `Enter` returns to menu after game over.
