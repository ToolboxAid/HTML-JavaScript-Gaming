Asteroids

Asteroids is a classic arcade game where you pilot a spaceship in a wrap-around field of drifting space rocks. Your goal is to shoot and destroy the asteroids and flying saucers while avoiding collisions.

Keys

1 or 2 starts one-player or two-player. Left/Right rotate, Up thrusts, Space fires, and Enter continues after game over.
