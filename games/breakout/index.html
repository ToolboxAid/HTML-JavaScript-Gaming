Breakout - Debug Showcase

Classic single-screen brick breaker debug showcase with crisp paddle control, rectangular arcade visuals, keyboard and gamepad support, and a minimal score-and-lives HUD.

Debug: OFF

Enable debug with ?debug=1, then use Shift+` for console and Ctrl+Shift+` for overlay.

When debug is enabled, Breakout auto-loads preset.gameplay and keeps overlay hidden until you open it.

Controls

Left/Right or A/D move the paddle. Space or Enter starts and launches. X returns to the menu. Gamepads use the left stick or D-pad to move, south button or start to launch, and east/cancel to return to menu.

Gameplay Loop

Break every brick to win the board. Each dropped ball costs one life, and three misses ends the run. Paddle motion adds english to the ball, so moving into the contact point can reshape the rebound angle.
