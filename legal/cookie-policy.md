# Cookie Policy

**Version:** 1.0

This Cookie Policy explains how Game Foundry Studio LLC uses cookies and similar technologies.

## 1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by a website. Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, and device identifiers.

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Keep you signed in
- Protect accounts and the platform
- Remember preferences
- Support creator tools and project workflows
- Improve performance
- Understand usage and errors
- Support payments or subscriptions, if enabled

## 3. Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are required for core service functionality. These may include cookies for:

- Login sessions
- Authentication
- Security
- Fraud prevention
- Load balancing
- User preferences needed for the service

You may not be able to use parts of the service without essential cookies.

## 4. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies may remember choices such as:

- Theme or display preferences
- Recently opened tools or projects
- Tool layout preferences
- Local editor settings

## 5. Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies or privacy-conscious analytics tools to understand how users interact with the service and improve Game Foundry Studio.

If analytics cookies are introduced, we should provide appropriate notice and choices where required.

## 6. Marketing Cookies

Game Foundry Studio does not currently require marketing cookies for the MVP legal foundation. If marketing or advertising cookies are introduced later, this policy should be updated.

## 7. Third-Party Cookies

Third-party providers may set cookies or similar technologies when they provide authentication, security, payments, analytics, hosting, or other services.

Examples may include Supabase, Cloudflare, Stripe, and similar providers.

## 8. Managing Cookies

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect login, security, preferences, or other service features.

If Game Foundry Studio provides a cookie settings interface, you may also manage non-essential cookie choices there.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted with a new Last Updated date.
