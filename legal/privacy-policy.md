# Privacy Policy

**Game Foundry Studio LLC**
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026
**Last Updated:** June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Game Foundry Studio LLC collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use Game Foundry Studio.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including:

- Account information, such as name, email address, username, and login details
- Project information, including game names, descriptions, settings, files, assets, and notes
- User Content, including games, text, images, audio, code, and other uploaded materials
- Communications you send to us
- Billing-related information, if paid services are enabled

## 2. Information Collected Automatically

When you use the service, we may collect technical information such as:

- IP address
- Browser and device information
- Pages visited
- Referring pages
- Session data
- Authentication and security logs
- Error and diagnostic information
- Cookie and local storage data

## 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for login sessions, security, preferences, and platform functionality. We may later use analytics cookies or similar tools to understand and improve the service.

See the Cookie Policy for more details.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide and operate the service
- Create and manage accounts
- Save and publish projects
- Authenticate users
- Secure the platform
- Prevent fraud, abuse, and misuse
- Provide support
- Improve tools, features, and performance
- Process payments, if paid services are enabled
- Comply with legal obligations

## 5. User Content and Published Projects

Content you publish may be visible to others. This may include game pages, creator pages, assets, descriptions, and other project information depending on your settings and platform features.

Do not publish information you do not want others to see.

## 6. Third-Party Providers

We may use third-party providers to operate the service, including providers for:

- Authentication
- Database and backend services
- Hosting and content delivery
- File and object storage
- Payments
- Email delivery
- Analytics
- Security and abuse prevention

Examples may include Supabase, Cloudflare, Cloudflare R2, Stripe, and similar providers.

## 7. Payments

If paid services are enabled, payment information may be processed by a third-party payment processor such as Stripe. Game Foundry Studio LLC may receive payment status, billing metadata, subscription status, and transaction records, but does not need to store full payment card numbers.

## 8. Sharing Information

We may share information:

- With service providers that help operate the platform

- To protect users, rights, safety, security, and the service

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, maintain security, and support legitimate business purposes.

Published content, backups, logs, and records may persist for a reasonable period after deletion or account closure.

## 10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Game Foundry Studio is not intended for children under 13. Users under the age of majority should use the service only with permission from a parent or legal guardian.

Depending on available features, you may be able to:
