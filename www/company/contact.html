The future support path for Creators, partners and press.

Creator Support

Support will be ticket-driven, organized and easy to follow.

Game Foundry Studio is preparing for an account-based support portal where Creators can open a support ticket, track requests and get help through the right channel.

Support

Open a support ticket for help with accounts, creator tools, publishing, learning resources or general platform questions.

Feature Requests

Open a support ticket to suggest tools, workflow improvements, creator features or community ideas.

Bug Reports

Open a support ticket with the page, steps, expected result and what actually happened.

Partnerships

Future partnership routing will support schools, creators, communities, platforms and organizations interested in game creation.

Press

Future press routing will provide official background, product updates and media contact handling without publishing personal contact details.

Creator Support Portal

The planned portal will connect support tickets to creator accounts, request history and clearer response expectations.
