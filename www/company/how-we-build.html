Creator Flow

A Creator starts with an idea.

That idea becomes a Game Design Document (GDD). The platform then helps transform that idea into structured game data and a playable experience.

Spark: Audio

Bring the game to life through sound.

Spark helps Creators bring games to life through sound.

  • Atmosphere

Learn, explore and keep moving.

Foundry Bot helps Creators learn, explore tools and overcome challenges.

  • Learning

  • Creator guidance
