Roadmap

What comes next for Creators, players and the shared Game Foundry Studio ecosystem.

Roadmap Direction

Creator value comes first.

The roadmap is organized around outcomes Creators can feel: better tools, smoother publishing, stronger community features and easier ways to collaborate and share.

Near Term

Make creation smoother.

  • Complete creator tools
  • Improve publishing
  • Improve creator workflow
Mid Term

Grow the creator ecosystem.

  • Community
  • Marketplace
  • Creator profiles
  • Ratings and reviews
Long Term

Open up bigger possibilities.

  • AI-assisted creation
  • Collaboration
  • Cloud publishing
  • Mobile support
Build · Play · Share

Every milestone should help Creators move forward.

Build improves the tools and learning path. Play improves testing and discovery. Share improves publishing, community and support for the work Creators make.
