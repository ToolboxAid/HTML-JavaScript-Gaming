# Community Guidelines

**Game Foundry Studio LLC**
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** June 24, 2026
**Last Updated:** June 24, 2026

Game Foundry Studio is built to help creators build, play, and share games. These Community Guidelines explain what is expected when using the platform, publishing projects, or interacting with others.

## 1. Be Respectful

Treat other creators, players, and community members with respect. Do not harass, threaten, bully, impersonate, or target others.

## 2. Keep Content Appropriate for the Platform

Do not upload, publish, or share content that is illegal, exploitative, abusive, hateful, sexually explicit, or otherwise harmful to the community.

## 3. No Hate or Harassment

Do not promote hate, violence, or dehumanizing content against people or groups based on protected characteristics or identity.

## 4. Respect Intellectual Property

Only upload or publish content you own, created, licensed, or otherwise have permission to use.

Do not copy games, assets, music, characters, brands, logos, or other creative works without the necessary rights.

## 5. No Malware or Harmful Code

Do not upload projects, files, scripts, or links that contain malware, spyware, credential theft, harmful redirects, unauthorized miners, or other malicious behavior.

## 6. No Spam or Abuse

Do not use the service for spam, scams, phishing, fake engagement, abusive automation, or attempts to manipulate platform systems.

## 7. Keep Accounts Honest

Do not impersonate another person, company, creator, moderator, or Game Foundry Studio representative.

## 8. Publishing Projects

Published projects should be safe for users to access and play. You are responsible for your project content, behavior, links, assets, and code.

Game Foundry Studio may remove or restrict projects that violate these guidelines or other platform policies.

## 9. Reporting Problems

If you believe content violates these guidelines, contact Game Foundry Studio with enough detail for review.

For copyright concerns, use the Copyright Policy or DMCA Policy.

## 10. Enforcement

Game Foundry Studio may take action when content, projects, accounts, or behavior violate these guidelines. Actions may include warnings, content removal, project restrictions, account suspension, account termination, or other steps needed to protect the platform.
