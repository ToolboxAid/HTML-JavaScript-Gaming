# Team Assignments

## Purpose

This file records active team assignments pulled from `BACKLOG_MASTER.md`.

## Rules

- A team pulls one item from `BACKLOG_MASTER.md`.
- The backlog item is marked `[.]` when assigned.
- The assigned item is placed under the owning team below.
- A team may only work on its assigned item.
- Each team may have one active assignment.
- Each team may have one active branch unless a MASTER override explicitly approves more.
