# Sample42 - Polished Playable Slice

## Purpose
Builds a cleaner mini-game slice on top of the evolved engine.

## What it shows
- prefabs
- render layers
- game modes
- interaction
- projectiles
- patrol AI

## Controls
- Enter: start/reset
- Arrow keys: move player
- KeyE: interact
- Space: fire projectile

## Behavior
Unlock the goal, avoid or disable the enemy, and reach the goal to win.
