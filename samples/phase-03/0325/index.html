Gravity

A small force-driven arcade build: one ball, constant downward pull, stable wall rebounds, a damped floor bounce, and simple start/pause/reset flow.

Controls

Left/Right or A/D gently influence horizontal motion. Space or Enter starts. P pauses. R resets. Gamepads use the left stick or D-pad for horizontal influence, south button to start, and start to pause.

Gameplay Loop

Launch the ball into a gravity-driven chamber and watch it accelerate downward, rebound off the side walls, and lose energy on each floor contact. This stays intentionally separate from Gravity Well and does not use orbital fields or ship controls.
