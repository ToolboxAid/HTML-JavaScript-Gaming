Multi-Ball Chaos

A beginner-friendly multi-ball motion game: pick a preset, start the chamber, and watch a clean bounded playfield stay stable under increasing ball counts.

Controls

1-4 switch presets. Space or Enter starts. P pauses. R resets. Gamepads use the south button to start, Start to pause, B/East to reset, and shoulder buttons to cycle presets.

Gameplay Loop

Use the 3 Balls, 6 Balls, 10 Balls, and Fast Chaos presets to compare how multiple balls behave in the same bounded arena. This build intentionally omits ball-ball collision, paddles, and gravity so the setup stays readable.
