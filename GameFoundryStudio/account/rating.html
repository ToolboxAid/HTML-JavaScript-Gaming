Account Reference

Rating quality checklist.

Static rating categories for game quality, content suitability, player experience, interaction patterns, and community safety review.

Game Rating

Core game quality, clarity of objectives, session flow, replay value, and overall game readiness.

  • Objective clarity
  • Progression quality
  • Session pacing
  • Replay value

Content Rating

Age suitability, themes, language, visual intensity, audio intensity, and marketplace expectations.

  • Age range
  • Theme intensity
  • Language review
  • Visual and audio sensitivity

Playability Rating

Controls, readability, difficulty curve, responsiveness, and ability to recover from mistakes.

  • Control feel
  • Difficulty balance
  • Readable feedback
  • Failure recovery

Interaction Rating

Input mapping, menu flow, player prompts, tutorial clarity, and multiplayer or shared interaction expectations.

  • Input mapping
  • Prompt clarity
  • Menu flow
  • Shared interaction quality

Safety / Community Rating

Community-facing quality, moderation expectations, identity safety, reporting needs, and player protection notes.

  • Community safety
  • Moderation needs
  • Reporting paths
  • Player protection

Overall Rating Summary

Combined score notes, release confidence, recommended fixes, and the final publish-ready rating decision.

  • Combined score
  • Release confidence
  • Recommended fixes
  • Final rating decision
